Hello! This is just a simple price tracker for cryptocurrencies created by me, Sean Mythen!

Data is retrieved from CoinGecko every minute.

Use your keyboard's LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys or your mouse's SCROLL wheel to browse the top 200 coins by market cap.

Use your keyboard's UP and DOWN arrow keys to change font size.

Press SPACEBAR to jump back to the 1st coin.

Press ENTER on your keyboard to toggle market cap ranking visibility.

DOUBLE CLICK anywhere to hide/show these instruction.
